Extra interpreter commands:
?suppress            Prevents output from being written to the terminal. This
                     is usually used in definition sets to hide output.
?unsuppress          Allows output to be written to the terminal.
?show_intermediate   Enables printing of intermediate terms during β-reduction.
?hide_intermediate   Disables printing of intermediate terms during β-reduction.
                     This is usually used in definition sets to hide output.
?print [words...]    Acts like echo in shell. This is usually used in definition
                     sets to provide extra information about its definitions.
?limit <limit>       Limits the number of β-reductions to be performed.
